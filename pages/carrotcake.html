Carrot Cake

This is a recipe from Shoot the Cook

For preparing this carrot cake, you will need the following

  • 4 eggs
  • 150gr of brown sugar
  • 200ml of oil
  • 300 gr of carrot
  • 250gr of flavour
  • 16gr of baking powder
  • 2 spoons of cinnamon powder

Once you have it all, it is very easy, just:

  1. Preheat the oven at 200 degrees Celsius
  2. In a bowl, mix the eggs with the sugar
  3. Add the flavour and the baking powder and keep mixing
  4. Add the cinnamon and keep mixing
  5. Add the carrot, previousli sliced. Mix it all until it os fully integrated
  6. Pour the mixture into a mould and put it in the oven at 170 degrees Celsius for about 45 minutes
  7. Eat and ejoy the best carrot cake of the world!!
