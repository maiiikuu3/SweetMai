Brownie with Caramel

This is a recipe from Alma Cupcake. I love all her recipes.

For preparing this brownie, you will need the following

  • 115gr of butter
  • 210gr of brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 120 gr of flavour
  • 160gr of black chocolate
  • 2 spoons of powder cocoa
  • 1 spoon of liquid caramel

Once you have it all, it is very easy, just:

  1. Preheat the oven at 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Melt the chocolate in a bowl
  3. Add the sugar and cocoa powder and keep mixing
  4. Add caramel and eggs and keep mixing
  5. Eat and ejoy the best brownie of the world!!
