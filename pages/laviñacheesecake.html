La Viña Cheescake

La Viña is a mitic basque bar in Donosti. It is very famous for their cheeascake, which is done in the oven.

For preparing this super cake, you will need the following

  • 200gr of cheese, the one you like the most, I use Philadelphia
  • 6 eggs
  • 200ml of cream
  • 100gr of sugar

Once you have it all, it is very easy, just:

  1. Preheat the ovet at 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Mix the eggs
  3. Add the cheese and keep mixing
  4. Add cream and sugar and keep mixing
  5. Pour the mixture into a mould and bake it for 30 minutes in the over
  6. Leave it until it's at room temperatura
  7. Eat and ejoy the best cheesecake of the world!!
